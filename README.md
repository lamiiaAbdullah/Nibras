# 🌟 Nibras Educational Platform

**Nibras** is an intelligent educational reference platform designed to centralize university course explanations, empower student content creators, and integrate an AI tutor to provide instant academic responses and personalized guidance. 

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
* **Centralize Content:** Address the issue of scattered educational materials across multiple platforms that causes confusion and wastes students' time.
* **Support Content Creators:** Provide a structured space for student creators to efficiently publish, organize, and track their educational resources.
* **AI-Powered Assistance:** Integrate an interactive AI tutor to deliver 24/7 instant academic support and personalized learning paths.

---

## 👥 User Analysis (Personas)
The platform architecture was designed based on a detailed study of three core user personas:
1. **The Knowledge Seeker (Student - Layan):** A high-achieving university student who needs quick, organized, and clear explanations to study effectively without information overload.
2. **The Content Creator (Student - Sara):** A knowledgeable computer science student looking for a dedicated dashboard to share files/videos, organize content by subject, and monitor student engagement metrics without repetitive explaining.
3. **The Smart Assistant (SmartGuide AI):** A 24/7 responsive AI assistant that adapts to individual learning styles, delivers concise guidance, and directs students to relevant resources.

---

## 📐 UI/UX Design Workflow

### 1. Low-Fidelity Prototyping
* Sketches mapping out the **Main Welcome Interface** and college categorizations.
* The **Content Creator Dashboard** showing file uploads and analytics (views, files, lessons).
* The **Student Course Explanation View** featuring video lectures and downloadable summary notes organized by channels.

### 2. High-Fidelity Prototyping
* Translated raw concepts into clean, modern, and interactive mobile user interfaces.
* Designed specific user flows including the dual-role **Login Page** (Student vs. Content Creator).

🔗 **Interactive Figma Prototype:** [Explore the Figma Design](https://www.figma.com/proto/5NpiAKz4Osq9PkZlp3u4Em/Untitled?node-id=1-2&starting-point-node-id=1%3A2&t=5JETVxgOt1eFGMdF-1)

### 3. Usability Evaluation
* Conducted testing with a sample of university students using a think-aloud protocol.
* **Key Findings:** Highly positive feedback regarding clarity, clean layout, and intuitive navigation.
* **Future Enhancements:** Adding descriptive visual labels for role selection on the login page and introductory tooltips for the AI features to maximize user confidence.
